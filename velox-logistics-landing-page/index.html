Frequently Asked Questions

How does the real-time tracking work?
We install proprietary IoT sensors on all containers that ping GPS coordinates via satellite every 10 minutes. This data is fed instantly into your dashboard.
What regions do you cover?
Velox operates in over 140 countries, with major hubs in North America, Europe, SE Asia, and the Middle East. We specialize in cross-border freight.
Do you handle customs clearance?
Yes! Our automated system generates the required documentation for 95% of goods. For specialized cargo, our brokerage team handles the filing manually.
